About Us

800Club Mathematica is one of the most eminent and actively functioning clubs of National Institute of Technology, Calicut. Our journey started in the year 2000 and since then we have been helping students to improve their skills in logic, reasoning and maths .Our events and activities manifest another level of mathematics beyond the academic level, which could help in achieving extra milestones in our future. We provide a marvellous platform for school as well as college students to unwrap their logical and reasoning skills and blossom their critical thinking.We also conduct regular mock tests for CAT,GATE and GMAT in our college to assist the aspiring students in their preparation. We join together with other clubs to host the Technical Week conducted exclusively for first years to enlighten and refresh their minds with challenging games and quizzes.
